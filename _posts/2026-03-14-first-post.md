title: "Why Languages Are Strange"
---

Languages behave in unexpected ways.

Some languages change meaning with tone, others change word order, and some even change words depending on who you are speaking to.

English itself is a mixture of many histories: Germanic structure, Latin vocabulary, French influence, and words borrowed from almost every corner of the world.

This blog is simply a place to write down observations like this — small notes about language, learning, and the patterns hidden inside words.
